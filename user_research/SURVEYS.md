# Research Methodology

## Online Survey (Quantitative)

**Target:** 50 participants with chronic health conditions  
**Duration:** 5-7 minutes  
**Distribution:** Health forums, patient advocacy groups, social media  
**Research Period:** February 2026

### Purpose
- Validate interview findings with larger sample
- Gather quantitative data on demographics and behaviors
- Identify patterns across broader user base
- Measure feature priorities

### Participant Recruitment

**Inclusion Criteria:**
- Age 18+ years
- Diagnosed with at least one chronic health condition
- Takes regular medication
- Owns a smartphone
- Willing to share health management experiences
